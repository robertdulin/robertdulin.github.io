Robert (Robbie) Dulin

PhD Candidate · Department of Economics · Princeton University

I am a fourth-year PhD student in economics at Princeton University and a Graduate Fellow at the Research Program in Development Economics (RPDE). My research examines how new ideas and technologies spread and take root in low-income countries — with a particular focus on how social networks and conceptual understanding shape learning and adoption.
